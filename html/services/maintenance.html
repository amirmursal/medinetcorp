Maintenance & Support

To remain competitive, equipment manufacturers and service providers must constantly re-optimize and augment their internal R&D resources. A significant opportunity for improved development efficiency is created when these companies migrate the support and maintenance of existing product lines and networks to Aricent.

Infrastructure Products and Devices
A clear best practice today among communications infrastructure manufactures is to shift the sustaining engineering and maintenance of mature product lines to Aricent. This strategic relationship – including responsibility for entire product lines – reduces overall costs, frees up internal R&D resources, and ensures exceptional stewardship of product portfolios. Additional opportunities often emerge for re-engineering to further reduce costs and extend the life of individual products and product families.

Service Provider Networks and Systems
Aricent takes a holistic approach to address critical business and operational challenges faced by our global service provider customers. This allows us to assist our clients in planning, developing, implementing and supporting efficient and scalable networks and operations including OSS/BSS solutions that enable streamlined processes, improve customer satisfaction and grow market share and revenue. Once your service or product or service has been developed, deployed, integration or tested, Aricent will continue to be there to provide ongoing maintenance and support to ensure your customers receive the quality service or product they anticipate.
