Development

Fidelis operates on the principle that application development should be delivered at a fixed price, on time, and within budget. To this end, we have been assisting our clients with the development of complex application software for over 3 years.

Fidelis Application Development Services include:
Custom web- and PC-based applications developed to meet specific business needs Web-enablement of existing and legacy applications.

Migration of existing and legacy applications from obsolete platforms to newer, cost-effective platforms

Application integration using Service Oriented Architecture (SOA) and Web Services Project Management for large-scale development efforts

Analysis and design, for new applications

Assessment of existing or in-development applications: architecture, design, and code quality

Coaching and mentoring of development staff in iterative and incremental software development methodologies such as RUP or Agile

Data Management and Reporting, including the development of Business Intelligence systems
